Research

Broadly, I do work in computational social sciences on social media. I study the behaviour and influence of agents on social media. Specifically, I work on a few themes: automated agents on social media (bots), influence maneuvers on social media, discourse analysis (hate speech, negotiation, stance expression), and social cybersecurity governance.

BotBuster Universe

Bots are automated agents on social media space. I work in the areas of bot detection and bot analysis. For greater detail, check out BotBuster Universe Project Page. Here's a selection of the bot studies:
  • BotBuster:Bot detection for Twitter and Reddit
  • BotBuster for Everyone: Bot detection for X Reddit and Instagram

Automated agents on social media

Automated agents such as bots and cyborgs live in the same social media ecosystem as humans. This research track forms the core of my PhD thesis, examining the interactions between the bot and human species, and the characteristics of bots on social media. Key papers in this area include:
  • Thesis proposal: The Bot Human Nexus on Social Media
  • Stabilizing a supervised bot detection algorithm: How much data is needed for consistent predictions?
  • Botbuster: Multi-platform bot detection using a mixture of experts


Influence maneuvers on social media

This pillar studies how people communicate, persuade and create communities on social media. Key papers in this area include:
  • Pro or Anti? a social influence model of online stance flipping
  • Online coordination: methods and comparative case studies of coordinated groups across four events in the united states
  • A combined synchronization index for evaluating collective action social media
  • Analyzing Public Opinion and Misinformation in a COVID-19 Telegram Group Chat(Best Paper Award)


Discourse Analysis

This thread falls unde a larger umbrealla of Natural Language Processing. I look at anything that generally has a social element to it. Key papers in this area include:
  • Is my stance the same as your stance? A cross validation study of stance detection datasets(Best Paper Award)
  • It Takes Two to Negotiate: Modeling Social Exchange in Online Multiplayer Games
  • How Hate Speech Varies by Target Identity: A Computational Analysis
  • I miss you babe: Analyzing Emotion Dynamics During COVID-19 Pandemic


Social Cybersecurity Governance

This thread looks at governance of social spaces and technologies, like social media and biometric technologies. Key papers in this area include:
  • How does fake news spread? Understanding pathways of disinformation spread through APIs(Most Cited Paper Award)
  • Digital Ethics for Biometric Applications in a Smart City
  • TikTok's Project Texas-Social Media Data Governance Across Geopolitical Lines
