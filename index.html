Exploring Creativity


I am a PhD student in Societal Computing at the Department of Computer Science in Carnegie Mellon University, supervised by Prof Kathleen Carley. My research interests are the study of the interaction between AI agents (bots) and humans on social media communities. My research methodologies involve computational social sciences and natural language processing. You can download my latest CV here. I received my Bachelors in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science of National University of Singapore, where I won the NUSS medal for Outstanding Achivement.

My Heart Transplant Story

I was born with Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy is a condition in which the heart becomes thickened But it means that I was easily breathless, and had lots of trouble doing even the most minute stuff like climbing a flight of stairs. Then my electrical rhythm started to fail. I had two pacemakers implanted since I was a teenager. I battled this for 29 years, the pain, the medications. One day in Feb 2023, I wasn't responding to the normal medications. On March 16 2023, my heart went into an abnormal rhythm for 39 days. After much failed medication attempts, I was admitted to UPMC Presbyterian hospital. There, I was blessed to receive a heart transplant. It is a whirlwind story, and I am still writing it.
